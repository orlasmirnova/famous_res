Famous Residents List

Welcome to the list of famous residents in Brighton.

Dave Gilmour

Well-known guitarist and played in pink floyd:

Cate Blanchett

When she renovated her house on Lewes Crescent, the whole road had to be closed off. She also has a residence in Crowborough, East Sussex.

Chris Eubank

World champion boxer who also featured in a Louis Theroux documentary.

Steve Coogan

Comedian who is also known as Alan Partridge. He has been spotted outside the ALDI on London Road, but it is unknown if he is still there now.

Noel Gallagher

It is said that Noel had a Brighton pad in the early 2000's, but it is unknown if he still frequents the area. You might spot him though!

Nigel Kennedy

Virtuoso violinist known for injecting punk influences into the classical music genre.

Fatboy Slim

Perhaps more synonymous with Brighton, Fatboy lived in Hove and now lives in Ditchling.
